Shipping Samples to NYX

1. Shipping Samples to NYX#

Ship your dewar to the following address:

National Synchrotron Light Source II
740 Brookhaven Avenue
Bldg. 740 - Loading Dock
Upton, NY 11973-5000
Attn: 19ID-NYX, Kevin Battaile

Be sure to include a FedEX Express return shipping tag. Return tags for FedEX Ground, UPS, DHL or other carrier may experience a shipping delay.

The following statement must be added to the air waybill or be on a separate sticker affixed to your dewar:

Not restricted as per Special Provision A152

We recommend that your write in permanent marker on the outside and inside lid of your dewar case:

Contact if Lost: <your name, address and phone number>

If the shipping tag is separated from your dewar this information will help the carrier contact you.
