2. Acknowledgements#

The following acknowledgement statements must be used in any publication that used data from experiments at NYX.

2.1. NYX Acknowledgement#

Use of the NYX beamline 19-ID at the National Synchrotron Light Source II
was supported by the New York Structural Biology center.

2.2. NSLS-II Acknowledgement#

This research used resources of the National Synchrotron Light Source II,
a U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) Office of Science User Facility operated
for the DOE Office of Science by Brookhaven National Laboratory under
Contract No. DE-SC0012704.

2.3. Eiger2 Acknowledgement#

This statement should be included for data collected through March 31, 2026

NYX detector instrumentation was supported by grant S10OD030394 through
the Office of the Director, National Institutes of Health.

2.4. Eiger2 Acknowledgement for PDB#

The following should be included in the ‘funding’ section of the PDB submission for data collected through March 31, 2026:

Funding Organization: Office of the Director, National Instutues of Health
Location: United States
Grant Number: S10OD030394
